What is an MC?

The Master of Ceremony, also popularly known as the MC or toastmaster, acts mainly as the host of an event, keeping it on track and ensuring its smooth flow all throughout. Although the content is very essential, other external factors can also contribute to the success of the event. One of these factors is the MC, therefore it is paramount that he or she must have experience in public speaking.

Why do I need an MC?

MC usually introduces participants or the artist who will soon perform on stage, in dialogue with the audience, and generally trying to keep the tempo of the show. Depending on the event hosted, an MC is sometimes required to bring а joke or anecdote.

HCC MC’s have a lot of working experience and will really bring your event to life.
