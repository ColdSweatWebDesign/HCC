Steve Cummins

Managing Director

When you are talking entertainment, entertainment management or events coordinating, there are not too many in the business that have the experience of our company’s founder and managing director Steve Cummins.

Steve has performed all around the world winning multiple awards in his field and has helped many of his peers and venue managers achieve their greatest goals.

Steve has the know how to make your experience with our company easy, efficient and exciting. With over 25 years’ experience in all facets of the industry, he will make any event shine.

Linda Thornton

Office Manager & Venue Consultant

Linda joined the company in 2008 and has had a major hand in the ongoing success of the company. Linda specialises in entertainment programming for venues and works one on one with all our contracted clients, ensuring the best service in the business.

As a musician, Linda has worked in very successful shows throughout Australasia and has a great understanding of both sides of the industry.

Paul Kelleners

Events, Staging Manage

With over 30 years of hands on experience and working with some of the biggest names in the entertainment world, Paul is the best man to have to take control of running events to time.

Prior to joining our team in 2013, Paul held the position of Entertainment & Production manager for Twin Towns Services Club for 28 years. Paul has received numerous awards for Best Production Team and knows if you want to be the best, you have to work with the best.

Stuart Durston

Sound Production Manager

It was once written “there is nothing you can’t do that can’t be done” and that has always been the attitude of our number one technician Stu Durston. Stu is a fully credited sound technician who bought 10 years’ experience with him when he joined the company in 2013.

Stu is truly tried and proven in his field and knows how to work efficiently under pressure; he is one who takes ownership of his responsibility and has even set the A.V and media outlets for the Prime Minister of Australia. Stu is also an accomplished musician and knows how to pull that sound.

Joel Molloy

Multi-Media & Marketing Manager

We believe it is the marketing of any event big or small that plays a major role in the success of anything we do and that is why we handpicked our man Joel and his team for they are specialist in this field.

Joel has a defined understanding of our clients’ needs and when it comes to letting people know about a product or event, we can tailor a quality package to suite any budget.

PRO-TEX strive to create a safe and happy environment for everyone to enjoy. With the absolute highest standard of excellence in training, it is no wonder why PRO-TEX sets the standards for security work on the Gold Coast.
