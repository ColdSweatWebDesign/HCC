DIRECTORS

Steve Cummins

Managing Director

When you are talking entertainment, entertainment management or events coordinating, there are not too many in the business that have the experience of our company’s founder and managing director Steve Cummins.

Steve has performed all around the world winning multiple awards in his field and has helped many of his peers and venue managers achieve their greatest goals.

Steve has the know how to make your experience with our company easy, efficient and exciting. With over 25 years’ experience in all facets of the industry, he will make any event shine.

Linda Thornton

Office Manager & Venue Consultant

Linda joined the company in 2008 and has had a major hand in the ongoing success of the company. Linda specialises in entertainment programming for venues and works one on one with all our contracted clients, ensuring the best service in the business.

As a musician, Linda has worked in very successful shows throughout Australasia and has a great understanding of both sides of the industry.
