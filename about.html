WHO WE ARE

Based on the Gold Coast, HCC Entertainment is an Australian wide production & entertainment company that showcases some of the best talent available. With fantastic live entertainment for any venue and production set ups, we can organise an event that will be remembered.

A professional entertainment service industry

With over 30 years of experience in the music industry Steve Cummins has a great knowledge base of production and shows. Steve has performed his award winning Niel Diamond Super Hits Show on an international stage and is world renown as the best Neil Diamond tribute show around. With a hand picked team, Steve Cummins created HCC Entertainment as a one stop shop for all entertainment and event management needs.

Need your venue set up?

HCC Entertainment also sets up any event for you. From corporate event management, to stage and lighting, security and even promotional material, we look after you. We have had experience with corporate events such as setting up a function for Tony Abbott and other local politicians. Feel free to enquire with us and we can work out some options for your event.

Plenty of entertainment for any venue

HCC Entertainment prides itself on the quality of their entertainers and event management. With huge feature shows and a handpicked events team we ensure your venue is set up and entertaining your guests in style and flawlessly. We have a large range of entertainment options including feature shows, live entertainment, comedians, DJ’s, MC’s, guest speakers, roving entertainment, kid’s entertainment and much more..

Got a special request?

At HCC Entertianment we are not limited to services that we can offer. If you have a special request that we can help you with, drop us a line and we will see what can be done.
